PeptidePal Privacy Policy

Your data, your device, your control.

Last updated: March 13, 2026

This document is a product-policy draft and not legal advice. Have counsel review before app store submission.

1. Who We Are

PeptidePal is operated by PeptidePal. For questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@peptidepal.app. For general support, reach us at support@peptidepal.app.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how PeptidePal handles information when you use the mobile app and related services.

3. Information We Process

3.1 Information You Enter

Depending on how you use the app, PeptidePal may store:

  • Personal logs (date/time, selected peptide or personal medication, dose text, notes)
  • Injection site selections
  • Protocol and reminder schedules
  • Favorites and preferences
  • Optional vial photo metadata and user-entered vial fields
  • Optional user-added personal catalog medications

3.2 App Configuration Data

The app stores settings such as disclaimer acknowledgment, onboarding state, theme preference, and content sync metadata.

3.3 Subscription Data

If you subscribe to PeptidePal Pro, your purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management service, to verify your purchase status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and purchase receipt — it does not receive your name, email, or health data. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.

3.4 Free Trial

New users receive a 30-day complimentary trial of Pro features. The trial start date is stored locally on your device. No account creation or personal information is required to begin the trial.

3.5 Diagnostics

PeptidePal may record technical errors locally for troubleshooting. No account sign-in is required for core app usage.

4. How We Use Information

We use information to provide app features (logs, reminders, protocols, calculator, favorites), maintain local preferences, verify subscription status, and diagnose technical issues. We do not sell personal information.

5. Where Data Is Stored

5.1 On-Device Storage (Primary)

Core user data is stored on-device using local app storage. This includes logs, reminders, protocols, favorites, and settings. This data does not leave your device unless you choose to share it.

5.2 Subscription Verification

Purchase receipts are verified through Apple and RevenueCat to confirm your subscription status. This is limited to transaction data and does not include your health or log data.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal log content with third parties for advertising. Limited technical processing may occur through:

  • Apple platform services (app delivery, in-app purchases, notifications)
  • RevenueCat (subscription receipt verification only)

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Because data is stored locally by default, deleting app data or uninstalling the app removes local records from the device. In-app delete/reset controls remove local user-entered data on that device.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards appropriate for a local-first app architecture. No method of transmission or storage is guaranteed 100% secure.

9. Children

PeptidePal is not intended for children under 13.

10. Medical and Safety Notice

PeptidePal provides educational content only and is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment guidance. Always consult a qualified clinician before making health decisions.

11. International Users

If you use the app outside your home jurisdiction, you understand that information may be processed according to the laws applicable to the app operator and service providers.

12. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

13. Contact Us

Privacy questions: privacy@peptidepal.app
